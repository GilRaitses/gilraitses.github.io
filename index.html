About Me

I develop computational frameworks that bridge machine learning and domain science, with a focus on neural signal analysis and marine behavioral ecology. My research extracts meaningful structures from complex signal data using custom algorithms and physics-informed models. I'm particularly interested in how self-supervised learning can reveal emergent patterns in time-series data from biological systems and environmental monitoring.

Current Work

  • Neural Circuit Analysis: Developing a novel statistical framework that extracts functional motifs from Drosophila larval connectome data, identifying synchronized firing patterns that correspond to specific sensory inputs.
  • Marine Behavioral States: Building a transformer-based model to identify and classify humpback whale behavioral states from high-resolution dive telemetry, enabling automated detection of feeding events from time-series acceleration data.
  • Biomimetic Sensors: Creating signal processing algorithms for bio-inspired acoustic sensors that filter environmental noise while preserving biologically relevant signals, mimicking the selective filtering observed in mammalian auditory systems.

Featured Projects

M★ Framework

Developed a behavioral analysis toolkit that identifies complex, nested activities in ecological time-series data. The system uses adaptive windowing to detect hierarchical patterns in whale dive telemetry, reducing annotation time while improving classification of overlapping behaviors.

Cetacean Dive Pattern Analysis

Developed a recurrent neural network that classifies diving patterns in tagged humpback whales, achieving 92% accuracy in identifying feeding dives based on time-series acceleration and depth data collected off the coast of Massachusetts.

Drosophila Sensory Circuit Mapping

Created a graph-based analysis pipeline for larval Drosophila brain connectome data that identified previously unknown functional circuits responding to specific environmental stimuli, published in collaboration with the Center for Neural Circuit Dynamics.
